The Science Behind Our Approach

AI Flashcard Generator is built on the foundation of cognitive science research, particularly the spacing effect and active recall principles:

  • Active Recall: Testing yourself on material is more effective than passive reading. Our flashcard system encourages active engagement with the content.
  • Spaced Repetition: Reviewing information at increasing intervals significantly improves long-term retention. Our algorithm optimizes review schedules based on your performance.
  • Elaborative Interrogation: Our AI generates questions that encourage deeper processing of information, leading to better understanding and memory encoding.
