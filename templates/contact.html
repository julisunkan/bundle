Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with AI Flashcard Generator?

Simply go to our homepage, paste your text or upload a PDF document, and click "Generate with AI" to create flashcards instantly. You'll need to configure your Google Gemini API key in the Settings page first.

Where do I get a Google Gemini API key?

You can get a free Google Gemini API key from Google AI Studio. The free tier provides generous usage limits for most educational needs.

Is my data secure?

Yes! Your flashcards and study progress are stored locally in your browser. Your API key is also stored locally and is never sent to our servers. Content you upload for processing is not permanently stored. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.

Can I export my flashcards?

Yes, you can export your flashcard decks in multiple formats including PDF, CSV, JSON, and Anki-compatible format. This allows you to study offline or import your cards into other study applications.

What is spaced repetition?

Spaced repetition is a learning technique that involves reviewing information at increasing intervals. Our app uses this scientifically-proven method to help you remember information more efficiently and for longer periods.

What file types are supported?

Currently, we support PDF files for upload and plain text input. We're working on adding support for additional file formats in future updates.
