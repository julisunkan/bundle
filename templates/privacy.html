Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 30, 2025

1. Information We Collect

DigitalSkeleton collects minimal information necessary to provide our service:

  • Website URLs: The URLs you submit for analysis and conversion
  • Technical Data: Website metadata, manifest files, and PWA assessment results
  • Usage Data: Build history, job status, and system performance metrics
  • Browser Information: User agent, IP address, and browser capabilities for service optimization

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To analyze websites and generate mobile app packages
  • Quality Improvement: To enhance our PWA analysis and package generation algorithms
  • Technical Support: To troubleshoot issues and provide customer support
  • Usage Analytics: To understand service usage patterns and improve performance

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Temporary Storage: Website analysis data is stored temporarily during processing
  • Secure Transmission: All data is transmitted over HTTPS encryption
  • Limited Retention: Generated packages are deleted after 7 days unless downloaded
  • No Personal Data: We do not store personal information from analyzed websites

4. Data Sharing

DigitalSkeleton does not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties, except:

  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or legal process
  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help operate our platform (under strict confidentiality)
  • Business Transfer: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale

5. Website Analysis Process

When you submit a website for analysis:

  • We fetch publicly available content from the provided URL
  • We analyze HTML structure, manifest files, and service workers
  • We do not store or access private user data from the analyzed website
  • We only process information that is publicly accessible

6. Cookies and Tracking

DigitalSkeleton uses minimal tracking technologies:

  • Session Cookies: To maintain your session and job progress
  • Local Storage: To cache analysis results and improve user experience
  • No Third-Party Tracking: We do not use advertising or analytics cookies

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request information about data we have collected
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data from our systems
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data in a structured format

8. Data Retention

We retain data for the following periods:

  • Website Analysis: 30 days for performance optimization
  • Generated Packages: 7 days or until downloaded
  • Build History: 90 days for troubleshooting purposes
  • System Logs: 30 days for security and performance monitoring

9. International Data Transfers

DigitalSkeleton may process data in different countries:

  • We ensure adequate protection through appropriate safeguards
  • Data processing complies with applicable international regulations
  • We implement standard contractual clauses for international transfers

10. Children's Privacy

DigitalSkeleton is not intended for children under 13:

  • We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13
  • If we discover such collection, we will delete the information immediately
  • Parents can contact us to request deletion of their child's information

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time:

  • Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date
  • Significant changes will be highlighted prominently
  • Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of changes

12. Contact Information

For privacy-related questions or concerns:

Email: privacy@digitalskeleton.com

Response Time: Within 72 hours

Data Protection Officer: Available upon request

Our Commitment

DigitalSkeleton is committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices. We only collect what we need to provide our service and never sell your data to third parties.
